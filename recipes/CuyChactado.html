Cuy Chactado

It is an emblematic dish of Peruvian gastronomy prepared differently in each region. The term comes from the Aymara verb chchactaña "to disappear" which refers to the meat that is roasted under a stone of approximately 2kg. Guinea pig meat is a food of excellent flavor and quality

Ingredients Needed

  • 2 guinea pigs.
  • ½ liter of oil.
  • Boiled potatoes.
  • Salt to taste.
  • Minced garlic.
  • Onion bramble and llatan.

Steps For Preparation

  1. Clean the guinea pigs well.
  2. Season them on both sides with garlic, salt and sunbathe them for a while.
  3. In a frying pan, heat the oil and fry the guinea pigs upside down, placing a chaquena (small stone of about 2 kg.), on top of the guinea pigs. So let them cook and brown.
  4. Once golden, add a little lemon and brown on both sides.
  5. It is served with golden potatoes, zarza and llatan.
