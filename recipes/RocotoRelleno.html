Rocoto Relleno


Among the large number of dishes offered by Arequipa gastronomy, one stands out for its intense flavor and great popularity: the stuffed hot peppers. Usually served as a starter stew, it is one of the most consumed and preferred not only by Arequipe√±os, but also by all Peruvians. And it is that nobody resists this delicious globular chili stuffed with minced meat and melted cheese.


Ingredients needed
  • 6 hot peppers
  • 1/2 kg ground beef
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 onion
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs
  • 2 olives
  • 6 slices of fresh cheese
  • 6 boiled potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon oregano
  • Oil, sugar, seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste


Steps For Preparation
  1. We begin by cutting a cap off the hot peppers, remove the veins and seeds trying not to break them, wash and boil it 2 times (for 3 minutes) in salted water, changing it each time.
  2. In the third time we boil in water with sugar, then place them and let them drain well.
  3. Chop the onion into cubes, fry it in three tablespoons of oil; when it begins to brown, season with salt and cumin, mix and add the ground meat, stirring it with a wooden spatula.
  4. Then, we fry a few minutes until the meat changes color. Season with oregano, salt and pepper to taste, cook for a few more minutes.
  5. Later, we add the chopped parsley, stir well and add the olives and chopped eggs. Mix and remove from heat.
  6. With the preparation of the filling already ready, we proceed to fill the hot peppers and place them in a baking dish, cover the hot peppers with a slice of cheese and place the lid that was removed at the beginning. Next to the hot peppers we place the peeled potatoes, sprinkle the hot peppers and potatoes with milk and then put them in the oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
